Cardiology

Comprehensive Heart Care Center

Our Cardiology Department is dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart and blood vessel diseases. We provide personalized care using advanced cardiac technology and patient-centered approaches.

Specialized Services

Cardiac Surgery

Our surgical team performs both major and minimally invasive heart procedures to restore normal cardiac function and improve patient outcomes.

Diagnostic Testing

We offer advanced diagnostic tools, including ECG, echocardiograms, and stress testing, to ensure accurate heart health assessments.

Preventive Care

Our preventive programs focus on lifestyle guidance, nutrition, and early detection to reduce the risk of heart disease.
