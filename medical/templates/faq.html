Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our hospital, medical services, appointments, and patient care. If you need more help, feel free to contact our support team.

  1. Home
  2. FAQ

How can I book an appointment with a doctor?

You can book an appointment online through our website by visiting the “Book Appointment” page. Alternatively, you can call our reception desk or visit any of our clinics during working hours.

Do you accept medical insurance for payment?

Yes, we accept most major medical insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card and identification during your visit. Our billing department will assist you with claim processing.

What should I bring for my first hospital visit?

Please bring a valid ID, any previous medical records or test results, and a list of medications you are currently taking. For insured patients, don’t forget your insurance documents.

Are emergency services available 24/7?

Yes, our emergency department operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We have a dedicated team of emergency doctors and nurses available to handle all urgent medical situations.

How can I get my lab test results?

Lab results can be accessed through our patient portal once they are available. You may also receive them by email or collect them in person at the hospital’s laboratory department.

Do you offer home healthcare or telemedicine services?

Yes, we offer both home healthcare visits and telemedicine consultations for patients who are unable to visit the hospital in person. You can schedule these services through our website or by calling our customer care line.
